Welcome to windaid institute!

WindAid Institute provides volunteer projects in Peru fro people taking a gap year, carrer break, returement or time out from education, employment or training.

Who are we serving with this website?

Volunteers who wish to come down to peru to learn to build wind turbines for rural areas without electricity. It targets all age groups but majority university students, young professionals, environmentalists and graduate students
